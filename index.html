Open source & local communities

Open Seattle advocates for open data and civic technology projects that improve the lives of Seattle residents. We host monthly meetups where members pitch and work on projects, and build relationships between tech workers, community groups, and local government.

Projects

  • seattle.io

    A collection of tools & resources for building Seattle-focused applications.

  • HeyDuwamish!

    A community of residents monitoring toxic waste cleanup & environmental health issues along the Duwamish River.

  • nwdata.org

    A directory of open civic data resources for the Pacific Northwest.
